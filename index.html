Whether you are a seasoned Perl programmer or a 'newbie', please feel free to participate on the KC perl mongers mailing list or join us at one of our irregularly scheduled meetings.

Meetings are generally the second Tuesday of each month at 7pm. We currently meet at Barley's Brewhaus in Shawnee at 16649 Midland Drive, the southeast corner of Midland and Renner, just east of I-435. There's a wider variety of food than at our previous meeting location, and they serve beer. Underage perl mongers are still allowed in, though, so don't let that keep you away. We usually get a few hightop tables in the back of the restaurant, past the bar to the left, near the pool tables. New members are always welcome!
